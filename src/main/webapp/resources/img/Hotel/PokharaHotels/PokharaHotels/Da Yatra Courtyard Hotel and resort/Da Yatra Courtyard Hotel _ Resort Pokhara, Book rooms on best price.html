Lakeside Pokhara

US$165 - US$424

About Da Yatra Courtyard Hotel & Resort

Da Yatra Courtyard Hotel & Resort is located in the heart of Pokhara. This spa hotel is within a 10-minute walk of Barahi Temple and Ratna Mandir. Seti Gandaki and Pokhara International Mountain Museum are also within 3 mi (5 km). This hotel features a full-service spa, a restaurant, and a nightclub. Free WiFi in public areas and free self parking are also provided. Additionally, a bar/lounge, a coffee shop/café, and a rooftop terrace are onsite. All 48 rooms provide conveniences like living rooms and refrigerators, plus free WiFi and flat-screen TVs with satellite channels. Guests will also find balconies or patios, minibars, and room service.

The hotel offers host meetings and conference halls which can accommodate 135-150 people in various formations. Enjoy gourmet food prepared by our chef and kitchen team served by amicable wait-staff. Complimentary self parking is available on site.
